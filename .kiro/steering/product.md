# Product Overview

**Codementee** is a freemium mentorship-based interview preparation platform that connects aspiring software engineers with experienced mentors from top product-based companies for mock interviews.

## Core Value Proposition
"Real mock interviews with engineers who've cracked product-based companies like Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and more."

## User Journey Strategy
**Freemium Model**: Experience First, Pay Later
- Users can register for free and explore the platform
- See actual dashboard, features, and pricing before committing
- Natural upgrade path through integrated booking + payment flow
- Higher conversion rates through value demonstration

## Key User Roles
- **Admin**: Platform management, user oversight, revenue tracking, system configuration, **mentor assignment and booking confirmation**
- **Mentor**: Conducts mock interviews, provides structured feedback, **receives assignments from admin**
- **Mentee**: 
  - **Free Tier**: Explores platform, sees features, browses pricing
  - **Paid Tier**: Books mock interviews, receives feedback, accesses all features, **receives mentor assignments via email**

## Business Model
- **Freemium Strategy**: Free exploration → Value demonstration → Natural upgrade
- **Transparent Pricing**: Foundation (₹1,999/1 mock), Growth (₹4,999/3 mocks), Accelerator (₹8,999/6 mocks)
- **Sustainable Unit Economics**: ₹800 mentor payouts per session with 40-60% profit margins
- **Payment Integration**: Razorpay (live) integrated into booking flow
- **Email Automation**: Resend for welcome and upgrade communications

## Core Features
- **Free Registration**: No payment barrier to entry, immediate platform access
- **Enhanced Booking System**: 5-step wizard (Company → Type → Slots → Confirm → Payment for free users)
- **Admin-Controlled Mentor Assignment**: Professional mentor matching by admin team, communicated via email
- **Interview Types**: Coding (60-90min), System Design (45-60min), Behavioral (30-45min), HR Round (30-45min)
- **Company-Specific Tracks**: Role-level preparation for Amazon (SDE, SDE2), Google (L3-L6), Meta (E3-E7), etc.
- **Difficulty Levels**: Junior, Mid-level, Senior, Staff+ with appropriate matching
- **Integrated Payment Flow**: Seamless upgrade during booking process
- **Enhanced Feedback System**: Structured post-interview evaluations with detailed metrics
- **Role-based Dashboards**: Different experiences for free vs paid users
- **Dynamic Pricing**: Admin-configurable pricing plans with transparent limits
- **AI Tools**: Resume analysis, interview prep assistance (paid tier)

## Target Companies
Focus on preparation for interviews at major product-based companies and Indian unicorns:
- **Product Companies**: Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Meta, Apple, Netflix
- **Indian Unicorns**: Flipkart, Zomato, Paytm, Swiggy
- **Role-Specific Tracks**: SDE levels, L-levels, E-levels, ICT levels with company-specific preparation
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: 10+ companies with detailed interview tracks and difficulty levels

## Conversion Funnel
1. **Discovery**: Landing page with clear value proposition
2. **Free Registration**: No payment required, immediate access
3. **Exploration**: Dashboard with features preview and pricing
4. **Intent**: Start booking process, select company/type/slots
5. **Conversion**: Choose plan and complete payment
6. **Mentor Assignment**: Admin assigns appropriate mentor and confirms booking
7. **Retention**: Full access to mock interviews with assigned mentor and feedback
